Adversity

Adversity is a 2 lane attack and defend Minecraft PvP map. Players battle to defeat their opponent's Pharaoh while also defending their own. The first team to defeat the opponent's Pharaoh will win! Cast rituals to summon resources, status effects, and enchantments. Lead your team to victory and overcome Adversity!

Eris

Eris is an action packed defence style Minecraft PvP map. In this asymmetrical battle, players are divided into invading and defending teams. The Invaders objective is to destroy the redstone core before it becomes fully charged. The core will take 15 minutes to charge, and once it is fully charged, the Defenders will win!

Quarry Carts

Quarry Carts is a simple escort/payload Minecraft PvP map. Two teams battle to escort their minecart payload to the end of their track. The first team to reach the end of their track with their payload wins. Players are outfited with protective armor, a sword, a bow, a shovel, and a pile of dirt to manage their defences and offensive pushes.
